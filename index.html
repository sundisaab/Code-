Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Courses programs

Who is eligible for these Courses?

Our Course are open to students pursuing any degree (BE/BTech, BSc, BCA, etc.) as well as recent graduates. Some advanced programs may require basic programming knowledge, which will be mentioned in the program details.

Are these Courses paid?

We offer both paid and unpaid Courses options. Paid internships are performance-based and available after initial evaluation. All interns receive certificates and project experience regardless of payment status.

What is the duration of Courses?

Course durations vary from 2 to 6 months depending on the program. Most web development internships are 2-3 months, while AI/ML programs typically run for 4-6 months.

Will I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, all Students who successfully complete their program receive a verified certificate that can be shared on LinkedIn and included in resumes. Certificates include details of projects completed during the internship.

What is the selection process?

Our selection process typically involves: 1) Application form submission 2) Basic assessment (varies by program) 3) Interview (for some programs) 4) Onboarding. We focus more on your willingness to learn than prior experience.
